How does our firm's hiring process work?



Cafeina has two main purposes, to perform contracting work and to create our own projects. There are two groups of people who work at Cafeina, project contributors and partners.

Project Contributors are the people who work solely on Cafeina's internal project. Their official positions are the poisitions they have within that project. So, for example, say Jane Monkeyfuzzle was in charge of finance at Mermyth, her official positon within Cafeina would be Jane Monkeyfuzzle, CFO of Mermyth.

Partners work on both internal projects and projects for clients, and would have the position title of "Partner at Cafeina."

You can apply by filling out this form. All you really need in order to be a project contributor is passion for an idea and approval from that project's team. In order to be a partner, you must first be a project contributor, and check the "looking for partnership" box on our form. After this, the cofounders will consult with you to see if you'd be a good fit.
